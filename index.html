Alone in Space: A Tribute to Babylon 5

Babylon 5 - A short overview of important events


Babylon 5 is a beautiful space saga around a space station in ... space. It is wonderful to see how the story develops. Please see below some important events.


  • 2256:After the dissappearance of spacestation Babylon 4, the Earth Alliance started one final attempt to create a space(station) of peace. Babylon 5 was taken operational on the 9th of March 2256 under Commander Jeffrey Sinclair, Lt. Commander Laurel Takashima and security chief Michael Garibaldi
  • 2257:Babylon 5 was happily awaiting the arrival of Kosh Naranek from the Vorlon home planet. However upon arrival he was poisoned by what appeared to be Commander Jeffrey Sinclair (identified by telepath Lyta Alexander). While he was trying to prove his innocence, chief of medicine Dr. Benjamin Kyle is searching for a way to save his life. In the end fortunately everything worked out, but the Lt. Commander, chief of medicine and Lyta Alexander where recalled back to Earth.
  • 2258:Babylon 5 now has a few new arrivals: Lt. Commander Susan Ivanova, chief of medicine Stephen Franklin and telepath Talia Winters. The main story line this year is the plot the current Earth president Santiago which finally was discovered by security chief Michael Garibaldi but it was too late. Michael Garibaldi was shot by one of his agents and the ship of the president exploded near moon Io. Some other events that happened:
    • Raider attack around B5 controlled space
    • First contact with a Soul Hunter
    • A rogue telepath almost destroyes the station
    • Babylon 4 reapeared and was sucessfully evacuated
  • 2259:Commander Jeffrey Sinclair has been recalled in the aftermath of the Earth president's assassination. He has now been replaced with Captain John Sheridan who secretely tries to prove that the vice-president Clark was behind the assassination. Meanwhile an ancient race known as the Shadows are emerging and ally themselves behind the scenes to the Centauri. Also happening:
    • Centauri start a war with the Narn
    • Michael Garibaldi wakes up from his injuries
    • A rogue telepath almost destroyes the station
    • Babylon 4 reapeared and was sucessfully evacuated
  • 2260:Babylon 5 declares independent from the Earth Alliance due to Earth's transformation into a dictatorship. Meanwhile the shadow war is full on and Captain Sheridon detonates strong nuclear bombs on the Shadow's homeplanet Z'Ha'Dum
  • 2261:Somehow Sheridan survived, ends the shadow war and frees Earth from its dictatorship. Everyone is happy and Captain Sheridan becomes president of the Interstellar Alliance
  • 2262:Captain Elizabeth Lockley now takes over as captain of Babylon 5. Other things happen as well.
    • Rogue telepath war
    • Drakh, former allies of the Shadows, take over the Centauri and start small wars everywhere
  • 2282:End of Babylon 5 - the space station has been shutdown by the Earth Alliance due to lack of budget
